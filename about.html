About

Predict the Sky was started at the first Space Apps Challenge, hosted at the Met Office back in 2012. The idea was to merge weather prediction data with the tracking of space objects so that you'd know how much of a chance you'd actually have to see something.

Since then, it's been worked on at several subsequent hack days, notably the 2013 Space Apps Challenge where this site was started and the API came centre-focus. The intention is to publish an API for space objects and weather predictions for other people to build upon.
